# Privacy Policy for Virtual PAPI

**Last Updated: November 17, 2025**

## Overview

Virtual PAPI is a privacy-first aviation navigation application designed to operate entirely on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy by not collecting, storing, or transmitting any personal information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Virtual PAPI does not:
- Collect any personal information
- Track your location remotely
- Send analytics or usage statistics
- Use third-party tracking services
- Store data on external servers
- Require user accounts or authentication

## Location Data

Virtual PAPI uses your device's GPS to provide visual approach guidance. This location data:
- Remains entirely on your device
- Is never transmitted to external servers
- Is never stored persistently
- Is only used for real-time navigation calculations

You can control location permissions through your device's Settings app at any time.

## Network Usage

Virtual PAPI is designed to work completely offline. The app includes optional functionality to receive location data from X-Plane flight simulator software via local network (UDP):
- This feature is entirely optional and disabled by default
- This feature only listens to UDP packets
- No packets are ever transmitted
- Communication occurs only on your local network
- No data is sent to or from the internet
- No external servers are contacted

## Third-Party Services

Virtual PAPI does not use any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Data Storage

All app data, including:
- Airport and runway information (pre-loaded database)
- User preferences and settings
- Application state

is stored locally on your device and never synchronized or backed up to external servers.

## Children's Privacy

Virtual PAPI does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
